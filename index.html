Why Plancord

Problems encountered in manual rostering

Every management needs more time to run their business. But they seem to be unable to avert the time spent in creating manual rostering for their employees. Plancord automates the entire rostering activities. But before we unveil what Plancord does exactly, here are some of the problems of manual rostering. You wouldn’t want to continue with these issues; it’s obvious.

  • Employees are left with no choice but to follow what the management draws out and imposes on them.
  • Inefficiencies are bound to occur while preparing duty rosters.
  • IA request for a swap with colleagues can mean altering the entire roster.
  • Constant check on rosters to avoid missing out on duty.
  • Management undergoes the stress of creating duty rosters.
  • Poor staff retention due to employee dissatisfaction from the inability to plan ahead.

About Infomaxis

Infomaxis Limited, Cork, Ireland is a brand committed to simplifying the works of employees and employers through efficient technologies. We’re the makers of the leading self-rostering app ideal for organizations and businesses that roster their staff. Plancord Mobile App is the spark that ignites fair life-work balance among employees, as well as takes the hassle of managing rosters off the shoulder of the management of shift workers.

Designed by nurses who have been through the arduous task of rostering, we realize the workload of creating duty rosters for employees while spending mundane hours and resources to make it happen. Plancord Mobile App automates everything you need about duty rosters for your brand. We make it easier for you to plan your daily life without hassle.
